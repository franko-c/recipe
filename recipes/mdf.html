Makaronia du Fournou

Makaronia du Fournou is a traditional Greek baked pasta dish with a rich tomato and meat sauce, layered with creamy béchamel and topped with melted cheese.

This dish is perfect for a cozy family dinner or any special occasion, blending savory flavors into one comforting meal.

A rich, savory, and comforting Greek baked pasta dish perfect for any occasion.

first steps:

Before you start cooking Makaronia du Fournou, there are a few essential steps to prepare. Start by boiling your pasta until just al dente, as it will continue cooking in the oven.

Prepare your meat sauce by cooking ground beef or lamb with onions, garlic, and tomatoes, creating a flavorful base.

For the béchamel sauce, make sure you have your butter, flour, milk, and cheese ready to go, as you’ll need to work quickly to prevent clumps.

Here’s a checklist to help you get organized:

  • Prep your ingredients: Boil your pasta and gather ingredients for the meat sauce and béchamel.
  • Cook the meat sauce: Sauté onions, garlic, and ground meat, adding tomatoes and seasonings.
  • Make the béchamel: Prepare the creamy béchamel sauce with butter, flour, milk, and cheese.
  • Grate the cheese: Prepare the cheese for topping and layering your dish.

you will need:

  • 400g pasta (penne, rigatoni, or macaroni)
  • 500g ground beef or lamb
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp butter (for béchamel sauce)
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour (for béchamel sauce)
  • 2 cups whole milk (for béchamel sauce)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese (for béchamel sauce)
  • 1 cup grated Kefalotyri or Kasseri cheese (for topping)

time to cook!

  1. Begin by boiling your pasta according to package instructions until just al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onions and garlic, and sauté until softened and fragrant, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add ground beef or lamb to the pan, cooking until browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, adding wine if using, until the sauce thickens.
  4. For the béchamel, melt butter in a separate pan over medium heat. Stir in the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes. Gradually whisk in the milk, cooking until the sauce thickens, about 5 minutes. Stir in the Parmesan cheese and season with salt and pepper.
  5. In a large baking dish, layer the pasta, meat sauce, and béchamel. Repeat the layers, finishing with a layer of béchamel and topping with grated Kefalotyri or Kasseri cheese.
  6. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) for 25-30 minutes or until the top is golden brown and bubbly.
  7. Let the dish rest for 5 minutes before serving. Enjoy your homemade Makaronia du Fournou!

additional tips:

Makaronia du Fournou is a classic comfort food, ideal for feeding a crowd or enjoying a cozy meal at home.

This dish can easily be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for a couple of days or frozen for later use.

Feel free to adjust the recipe with different cheeses or by adding extra herbs like basil or thyme to the meat sauce for additional flavor.

If you prefer a vegetarian version, substitute the meat with mushrooms or lentils, and use plant-based cheeses and milk alternatives for the béchamel.
